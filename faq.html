Payment & Insurance

We work with a variety of insurance providers for medically necessary dermatology services. Coverage may vary depending on your plan and treatment. Please contact our clinic to verify your insurance benefits before your appointment.
Yes, we offer flexible payment options for selected treatments and procedures. Our team can discuss available payment plans and financing solutions during your consultation.
Treatment costs vary depending on the procedure, treatment area, and number of sessions required. We provide transparent pricing and a personalized treatment plan during your consultation.
Insurance coverage for consultations depends on your provider and the reason for your visit. Medical dermatology consultations may be covered, while cosmetic consultations are typically self-paid. Our staff can help you understand your coverage options.

Aftercare & Recovery

After your treatment, follow the personalized aftercare instructions provided by your dermatologist. This may include avoiding direct sun exposure, using recommended skincare products, staying hydrated, and refraining from harsh exfoliants until your skin has fully recovered.
Recovery time varies depending on the treatment performed. Many non-invasive procedures have little to no downtime, while more advanced treatments may require a few days to several weeks for complete healing. Your dermatologist will discuss expected recovery times during your consultation.
Most treatments are safe and well-tolerated. Temporary side effects such as mild redness, swelling, sensitivity, or peeling may occur depending on the procedure. These effects are usually short-lived and resolve naturally as the skin heals.
